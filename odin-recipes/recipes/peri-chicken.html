This Peri Peri chicken is made with the chef's take on African peri peri sauce using fresh and dried chiles. The marinade is incredibly flavorful and gives the chicken a beautiful color as well.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Chicken Leg/Thigh Quarters
  • Peppers
  • Lemons
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Blend peppers with sugar, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Pour 3/4 of the marinade into resealable plastic bag set in a bowl. Reserve the rest for serving on the side.
  3. Make shallow cuts on each chicken piece side. Marinate chicken with paprika and other spices to your liking.
  4. Set chicken skin side into the grill and turn them every 5-12 minutes.
  5. Serve with left over marinade.
  6. Enjoy!
